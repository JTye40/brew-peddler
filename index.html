Coffee

We take pride in delivering high-quality coffee! We roast our own beans, craft all our flavors in-house, and source ingredients locally. Our goal is to positively influence Spokane’s coffee culture, utilizing exceptional local products like honey from Tates Honey Farm and produce and dairy from regional farms.

Eats

Our menu features house-made pastries and light café fare, with a focus on seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. While our offerings may be small, we believe that quality and creativity speak volumes. We actively engage with local farmers through regional markets to ensure our menu reflects the best of what’s in season.
